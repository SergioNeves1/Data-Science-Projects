Przemek Chojecki is an AI entrepreneur with a PhD in mathematics and a member of Forbes 30 under 30 list in Poland. He did his PhD in Paris at Universite Pierre et Marie Curie, then became a Research Fellow and a lecturer at the University of Oxford. After returning to Poland, he took up research on artificial intelligence and mathematics, and founded a technological group ulam.ai, where he is the CEO.
