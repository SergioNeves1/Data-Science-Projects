Published on Nov 26, 2018

In this video we'll walk through deploying a Mac App Store app with Jamf Pro.

In this video we’ll be using a device-based deployment method. For this process, you’ll first need to have purchased a managed app license for the app in question through the Volume Store in Apple School Manager or Apple Business Manager.

If you haven't yet integrated your Jamf Pro instance with Apps and Books, please see our video on Integrating with Apps and Books here: https://youtu.be/SiYjPB4D5ns

Please see the following Knowledge Base article for help determining if your applications are device-assignable: https://www.jamf.com/jamf-nation/arti...

For more information on the additional requirements and capabilities of managed apps, see the Jamf Pro Administrator’s Guide: https://www.jamf.com/resources/produc...

For more information on this process, see our documentation here: http://docs.jamf.com/jamf-pro/adminis...
