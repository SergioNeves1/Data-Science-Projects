Published on Aug 7, 2019

In this video I highlight my favorite ways to get data science experience without having a data science job. Many people are interested in working in data science, have the technical skills, but don't meet the work experience requirements set by most companies. The following are my ways to circumvent this:

#DataScience #DataScienceExperience #DataScienceJobs #DataScienceCareers
1) Research opportunities at your university
2) Working on personal projects that you share with your community
3) School projects / Schoolwork
4) Internship work
5) Contracting websites (Upwork, etc.)
6) Work for free at a non-profit or local business
