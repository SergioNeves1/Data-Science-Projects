Join me for this free Udemy class:

https://www.udemy.com/course/explore-...

I just released a new free class on Udemy about plotting population pyramids and building web applications around them. A Population Pyramid is a graph that quantifies males versus females by age segments. This is a very important concept in data science as it helps us understand where we're coming from and where we're going at any granularity - from the entire world down to a zip code. Let's see where to get great data and explore a few of these concepts.

https://www.udemy.com/course/explore-...

We end the class by building a cool mobile web application so the entire world can enjoy the fruits of your labor, and I'll even show you how to make matplotlib animations! So much fun!
