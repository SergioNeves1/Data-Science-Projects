Published on Dec 10, 2019

Josh Patterson, Director of Engineering of our RAPIDS software libraries for data science, provides his perspective about #datascience and the opportunity for data scientists to have a positive impact on society. He talks about NVIDIA’s culture of solving challenging problems and giving opportunities to employees of all backgrounds to do their life’s work.
