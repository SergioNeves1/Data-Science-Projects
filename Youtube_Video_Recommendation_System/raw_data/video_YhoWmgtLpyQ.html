In this video, Kyle explains if freelancing as a data scientist is worth it and how to know if a career in data science as a freelancer is right for you!

📊 Need help landing your first job as a data scientist? Check out my free resources below!

👉 Watch this if you want a job in data science: http://dsdj.co/free-training-yt
👉 Download our free technical skills guide: http://dsdj.co/skills-guide-yt
👉 Join my email community for the weekly tip: http://dsdj.co/email-tips-yt

The biggest thing with data science freelancing is you need to find yourself opportunities and work out all of the business technicalities. You need to do much more marketing and sales for yourself when you work as a freelancer compared to just finding a job you love where you only need to sell yourself once to get the job opportunity.

Working as a freelancer is great and there can be a lot of opportunities but the challenge is, first of all, can you do the work people hire you for independently, and secondly, can you sell yourself over and over again.
