Published on Aug 14, 2019

Linear algebra is the branch of mathematics concerning linear equations such as linear functions and their representations through matrices and vector spaces. Linear algebra is central to almost all areas of mathematics. In this course you will learn most of the basics of linear algebra which will help to understand better and apply in ML as well.
