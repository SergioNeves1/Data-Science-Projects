Abhini Shetye | Student Diaries | Success Stories | Data Science | GreyAtom School

Before joining GreyAtom, Abhini worked in the telecom. She dealt with people, gathering requirements and creating documentation and presentations. However, her interest lay more in the technical side of things, and she wanted to do something that helped businesses grow.


Although knew about Data Science, it wasn’t until she read more about it that she realised that it was a perfect fit for what she wanted to do in her life.

When choosing the right program, she shopped around at various institutes and zeroed in on GreyAtom because she liked the way the program exposed her to variety of technologies: data science, machine learning, NLP, deep learning, and big data, among others.


She then saw that the mentors were industry experts, and they were actual data scientists themselves. They brought practical experience into their sessions, in addition to the theory.


On GLabs, she liked the ranking feature which she felt motivated learners to do better every time.


Abhini’s transition from telecom to finance was a huge deal for her, and she credits GreyAtom with the successful outcome.
