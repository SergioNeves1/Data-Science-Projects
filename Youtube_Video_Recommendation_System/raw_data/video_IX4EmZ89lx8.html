With Acronis Snap Deploy (https://goo.gl/nZLFhS), you can redeploy an image to live Windows Machines, eliminating the need for manual booting of each individual system. You can also manage machine-specific configurations (including machine names, IP addresses, domain membership, and other settings). Acronis Snap Deploy will also take care of Windows Settings after deployment is complete.

If you support a large enterprise, an educational institution, or a government agency, you know that deploying new software and configurations to multiple systems is a complicated, time-consuming task. Acronis Snap Deploy is a complete, user-friendly solution that lets you provision all your workstations and servers like you are provisioning just one!

Fast and easy. Powered by the Acronis AnyData Engine, this product is ideal for rapid bare-metal deployments to a large number of PCs or servers, as well as effortless on-going redeployment to the same hardware.
