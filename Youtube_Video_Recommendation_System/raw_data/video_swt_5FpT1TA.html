Published on Dec 29, 2016

Ventev’s Rapid Deployment Kit allows setup of a mobile wireless network. This kit provides a ruggedized temporary Wi-Fi solution, supporting voice, video, and data access across a large area. The Rapid Deployment Kit includes a tripod with an extension pole, mounting hardware, data cables, quick start guide, and a powered enclosure that includes 10 hours of primary power (PoE+). (AP Not included)
