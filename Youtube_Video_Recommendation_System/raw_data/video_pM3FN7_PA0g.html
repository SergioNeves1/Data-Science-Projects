Data Science learning path is an excellent fast-track and hybrid program for students and working professionals looking to build their career in data science from scratch.

The program has been crafted with industry experts and top recruiters making it India's first Job oriented training program. The goal is to ensure that you are well equipped with knowledge, practice, interviewing skills to crack your dream job in this field. Concepts are clearly explained with the help of lectures, interesting activities, case studies and quizzes to give you hands-on exposure.

You will learn all the in’s and out’s of data science which includes basics of data science, analytics, Statistics and maths for data science, machine learning, data engineering, and deep learning.
