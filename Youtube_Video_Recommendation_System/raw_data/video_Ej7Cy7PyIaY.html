Published on Feb 14, 2020

In this presentation, Claudia Megele, Head of Service for Quality Assurance and Practice & PSW with Hertfordshire County Council, and Chair of the Principal Children & Families Social Worker (PCFSW) Network provides a perspective from the point of view of a practitioner on the risks that using data science could present. She provides comments on the report Ethics of Machine Learning in children’s social care, produced by The Alan Turing Institute and the Rees Centre, University of Oxford for What Works for Children’s Social Care.

The report is available at: https://whatworks-csc.org.uk/research...
