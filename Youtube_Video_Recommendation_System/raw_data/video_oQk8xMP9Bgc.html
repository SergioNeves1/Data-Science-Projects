"Leverage Classifiers with Machine Learning and AI" by Maxwell Ogunfunwa
Machine Learning and A.I. have been dominating our world of programming for years now. As the industry shifts focus to data the needs of understanding and processing data increase drastically each year. Maxwell will be sharing a technique he has used in his career to process and classify text, specifically, he will be using Netflix movie & tv-show reviews to predict their genre. The talk will be focused on how to leverage classifiers to organize data.

Recorded at The District Tap in Indianapolis, IN on 02/11/2020
