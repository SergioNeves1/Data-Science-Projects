Published on Mar 28, 2020

This lecture discusses three main components of machine learning,

1. data consisting of data points with feature and labels,

2. hypothesis space of feasible predictor functions and

3. loss function to measure prediction error.

These concepts are illustrated for an application revolving around a nice ski day in Finland.
