Machine Learning in the Radiotherapy Workflow: A Review of Modern Applications and Limitations of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in Radiation Oncology
Presented by UWA Medical Physics students:
- Nathaniel Barry
- Dane Lynch
- Jake Kendrick
- Brani Rusanov

A presentation outlining the various uses for machine learning along the external beam radiotherapy workflow. From creating synthetic CT datasets from MRI images to automated contouring. Providing insight into the potential future of AI in radiation oncology.

SCGH Cancer Centre (DD Block), Meeting Room 1
