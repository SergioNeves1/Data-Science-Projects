Published on Feb 12, 2020

Listen to these students, who’ve all come from different academic backgrounds, unanimously taking a step towards Upskilling with Jigsaw Academy’s Postgraduate Diploma in Data Science. While someone has the hunger to learn more to be able to stay relevant in the market, the other is looking at climbing the corporate ladder with the help of this newly acquired skillset.

About the Program:
Jigsaw Academy has partnered with the Manipal Academy of Higher Education for the Postgraduate Diploma in Data Science. It’s a full-time, 11-month course with in-person sessions held in Bengaluru and Hyderabad. The course also comes with Guaranteed Placement.
