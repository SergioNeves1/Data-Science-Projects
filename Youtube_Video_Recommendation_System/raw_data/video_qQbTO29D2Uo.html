Published on Mar 27, 2020

Kaggle has released a new competition for $100,000 total prize money. In this series we will work together on this competition from start to finish, learning some of the practical tools of machine learning and data science.

Timestamps:
Why top down: 0:30
ARIMA_td benchmark: 0:48
Overview of the code: 1:24
Main changes to the code: 4:20
Leaderboard result: 8:12

In part 5 of this series we will use a top down method that first makes forecast for a higher level serie (I'm testing with all levels from 1-9) and then distribute that forecast to its level 12 components. If this works it will be great because we might be able to do time series forecast for fewer series and then distribute later (transforming all level 12 series to the desirable time series format has been a real pain..)
