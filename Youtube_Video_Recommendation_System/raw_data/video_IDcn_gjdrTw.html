Scalars and Vectors in Data Science, A scalar is a physical quantity that has only a magnitude (size) and vector is also a physical quantity but having both magnitude and direction. In Linear Algebra, data is represented by linear equations, which are represented in the form of matrices and vectors, its concepts are crucial for understanding the theory behind Machine Learning especially with Deep Learning Algorithms. Machine learning algorithms often rely on certain assumptions about how data being mined. Ensuring that data meet these assumptions can be significant portion of the preparation work before model training and then prediction begins. Vector spaces is a neat tool for organizing the data and requires more than getting all the data into a table. #scalar #vector #machinelearning #datascience
