Published on Sep 17, 2019

GRA Partner Carter McNabb discusses machine learning (ML) and artificial intelligence (AI), and the significant opportunities for supply chains – specifically in relation to demand planning and inventory optimisation as well as sales, inventory and operations planning (SIOP).

He looks at what’s real and what’s hype in 2019 – as well as what systems and toolsets are available with inbuilt ML and AI capabilities to help improve demand planning outcomes.

Following is a demonstration of GAINS advanced planning system by GRA Partner Luke Tomkin and GRA Manager Nathan Singhavong. The demonstration takes the audience through the three essential modules of the system: Demand Planning, Inventory Optimisation and Replenishment Planning and how ML and AI are being used in these areas.

GAINS was recently rated as the leading Supply Chain Planning (SCP) toolset in Gartner Research’s 2018 Magic Quadrant report.
