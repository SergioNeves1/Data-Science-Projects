Published on Feb 14, 2020

This Panel discusses the broader questions around the ethics of using machine learning in children’s social care at the launch event for the report Ethics of Machine Learning in children’s social care, produced by The Alan Turing Institute and the Rees Centre, University of Oxford for What Works for Children’s Social Care.

The panel members:

Michael Sanders, Executive Director, What Works for Children’s Social Care
Ellie Ott, Research Fellow, Rees Centre, University of Oxford
David Leslie, Ethics Fellow, Public policy programme, The Alan Turing Institute
Kathy Evans, Chief Executive, Children England
Claudia Megele, Head of Service for Quality Assurance and Practice & PSW, Hertfordshire County Council

The report is available at: https://whatworks-csc.org.uk/research...
