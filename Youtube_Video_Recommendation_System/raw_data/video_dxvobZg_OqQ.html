Learn more about HSE University’s new online Master of Data Science on Coursera in this admissions webinar.

For a Russian version of the webinar, visit: https://youtu.be/i8jbhCxz_vk

https://www.coursera.org/degrees/mast...
Ready to become an expert in data science? Apply for the first fully English-taught online Master of Data Science from a top-10 Russian university, featuring applied projects with industry partners like Yandex. The Master of Data Science from HSE prepares students for successful careers in fields like analytics, data science, machine learning and artificial intelligence, research, and academia. Prospective students interested in this degree can learn about admissions requirements, opportunities for students, tuition, and program curriculum by watching this webinar.

This webinar aired on November 20, 2019.
